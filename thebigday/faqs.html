What should I wear?

This is a formal event, but above all else, we want you to be comfortable!

What canapes will be served during the welcome drinks?

There will be petite croque messieurs, sticky teriyaki beef ribeye skewers, veggie bean chilli tostada topped with guacamole and sundried tomato & Grana Padano arancini balls. For a full list of ingredients, allergens and dietary information, please see the full list of ingredients and allergens. If there is nothing that meets your dietary requirements, please email us and we can sort something out for you with the caterers.

What are the options for the wedding breakfast?

You will have a choice of starter, main course and dessert.

Where is Michelham Priory?

The venue's address is Michelham Priory, Upper Dicker, Hailsham BN27 3QS, United Kingdom. The area is fairly well connected by train - there are several train stations in the area between Lewes and Eastbourne.

Where can I stay?

There are Premier Inn and Travelodge hotels in nearby Eastbourne, Lewes, and Hellingly. Michelham Priory also has some suggestions of accommodation.

How should I get to and from the wedding?

We recommend using a local taxi firm to hire transport to and from your accomodation. When booking a taxi, please keep in mind that carriages is at 11:30pm.
